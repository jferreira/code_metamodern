What's Inside?

Killer Interactions is an educational guide and resource hub of every visual design skill needed for interface design, great for those that don't have a design background. Perfect for those who want to further themselves within screen-based design.

Teaching Approach

Below are a few potential personas. Let’s see if you one of these might fit your current position.

Learning Loops

Content • Exercise • Critique

Content is not enough, True knowledge comes from experience. Throughout this course, you will practice your knowledge and get vital feedback. So, that you understand what works & what doesn't.

Compound
Knowledge

Structured Layers

Go from zero knowledge, and gradually apply each simple layer of knowledge upon the next to apply compound effect.

Peer Growth

Collaborative Approach

As you learn, test your comprehension, and ideas with those you are learning with. Both bolster collaborative skills and test what you truly know with others.

Is this for you?

Below are a few potential personas. Let’s see if you one of these might fit your current position.

Complete Newbie

Zero Knowledge

If you know nothing of design and never worked within software products and want to change your profession this course will give you the skills to do so.

Graphic Designer

Print  Background

If you know how to design, but want to learn how to design for screens this course will give you the skills to do so.

User Experience Designer

Master of Flow

If you know how to design user experience's that people love, but your eye for detail is lacking, this course will give you the framework to create pixel perfect designs.

FE/BE Web Developer

React Master

You know how to create fantastical efficient and functional software, but you lack the understanding of where to start in crafting purposeful and beautiful user interfaces, this course will get you to give you a roadmap to do so.

Project Manager

Business Builder

You love to create or work with new business models, but your eye for desirable experience is off this course will train your eye and give you the skills to craft your own product prototypes, which you can use to perhaps convince investors.

Product Designer

Value Focused

You love understanding how things and people work, and enjoy creating value, but you may have found it hard to execute some ideas at the pixel level, this course will give you the tools, and technics to broaden your toolkit.
